Starting a discard pile

 
    If a word card is the first one turned up from the DRAW pile, this is what happens.
All cards through 9 Face value
 
   Draw Two =
    The player to the left of dealer must pick two cards and then the next player goes.
  
   Reverse =
    The dealer plays first, but then goes to the right instead of the left.
  
   Skip =
    The player to the left of dealer doesn't start (get it, he's skipped). The player to the left of him starts.
 
   Wild Card =
    The player to the left of the dealer calls out a color – then plays.
  
  Draw Four =
    It's put back in the deck and another one is chosen.

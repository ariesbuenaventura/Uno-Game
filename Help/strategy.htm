Strategy

  • Play high numbered cards first.
  • Use the Draw4 and Wild cards to change the color to the one with the highest valued cards in your hand.
  • Get rid of Draw4, Draw2 and Wild cards as soon as possible. The points will make you loose very quickly.
  • Remember what color your opponents had to draw a card on, you can sometimes use this to your advantage by getting rid of more cards in that color.
  • Remembering the color your opponent had to draw on, and saving one of that color, and a Wild4 also presents its self occasionally.
